Our Story

We specialise in good company, great kai (food), and exceptional kawhe (coffee).

Our motto means, "Nourish your body with organic food" and that is exactly what we aim to do - feed our guests with high quality organic produce and a whole lot of aroha (love).

Focused on the Māori practices of kaitiakitanga (guardianship and sustainability) and manaakitanga (hospitality), we pride ourselves on locally-sourced ingredients that are sustainably packaged from farm to plate.
