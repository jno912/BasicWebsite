Koru Kawhe

Nau mai! Haere mai! Welcome to Koru Kawhe

We specialise in good company, great kai (food), and exceptional kawhe (coffee).

Our motto means, "Nourish your body with organic food." Situated in the heart of the Waikato, close to many tourist attractions such as Mt Pirongia and the Waitomo Caves, we offer an authentic experience of Aotearoa New Zealand cuisine and language.

Good company, great kai (food) and exceptional kawhe (coffee). We are a local cafe focused on the Māori practices of kaitiakitanga (guardianship) and sustainability.
